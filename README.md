# Car Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel & Cognos)

## Overview
This project presents a sales and profitability analysis for a fictional car dealership chain, **SwiftAuto Traders**.  
The objective is to analyze historical car sales, profits, and service-related indicators, and to communicate insights through clear visualizations and dashboards.

The project simulates a real-world business scenario where data-driven insights are required to support managerial decision-making.

---

## Business Objective
The main goal of this analysis is to help management:

- Understand sales performance by dealer and car model  
- Identify profit trends over time  
- Compare performance across years and regions  
- Monitor service-related indicators such as recalls and customer sentiment  

The final output is designed to be easily interpreted by non-technical stakeholders.

---

## Tools and Technologies
- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Data preparation
  - Aggregations and calculations
  - Charts and pivot-based analysis
- **IBM Cognos Analytics**
  - Interactive dashboards
  - KPI visualization
  - Business intelligence reporting

---

## Data Description
The dataset contains historical information related to car sales and service performance, including:

- Dealer ID
- Car model
- Quantity sold
- Profit
- Sales date (year and month)
- Recall information
- Customer sentiment indicators

The data represents a structured sales environment typical of retail automotive businesses.

---

## Key Analyses and Visualizations

### Excel Analysis
The Excel workbook includes:

- Quantity sold by dealer (sorted bar chart)
- Profit trends by date and car model (line chart)
- Profit comparison by year and dealer (column chart)
- Profit analysis for a specific car model across dealers

These visualizations focus on identifying patterns, trends, and performance differences across dealers and time periods.

---

### Cognos Dashboards
The Cognos dashboards are organized into two main sections:

**Sales Dashboard**
- Total profit (formatted in millions)
- Total quantity sold
- Average quantity sold
- Quantity sold by model
- Profit by dealer (sorted comparison)

**Service Dashboard**
- Number of recalls by car model
- Customer sentiment distribution (positive, neutral, negative)
- Monthly comparison of quantity sold vs. profit
- Heatmap of recalls by model and affected system

The dashboards provide a consolidated view of both sales performance and service quality indicators.
